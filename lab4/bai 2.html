From Garden to Plate

A potager is a French term for an ornamental vegetable or kitchen garden. Often flowers (edible and non-edible) and herbsare are planted with the vegetables to enhance the garden's beauty. The goal is to make the function of providing food aesthetically pleasing

What to Plant

Plants are as much for their functionality as for their color and form. Mary are trained to grow upward. A well-designed potager can provide food, cut flowers and herbs for the home with very little maintenance. Potagers can disguise their functionof of providing for a home in a wide array of foorms - from the carefree style of the cottag garden to the formality of a knot garden.
